Behind the Code

Born in London and raised in New York, I spent a lot of time playing sports like football and lacrosse. Alongside being active, I found myself drawn to computer science. After attending multiple camps as well as exploring different coding languages on my own, I discovered a passion for programming. Coding to me was (and still is) such a captivating challenge, a complex puzzle that satisfied my love for logic. Coming from both a sporty and tech-based background, it's the blend of hands-on team spirit along with logic-based problem solving that makes me unique in the industry.

During my free time, I find balance in family time, going to the gym, and playing guitar. In a world that's increasingly digital, I strive for a harmonious blend of technology, creativity, and personal growth.
